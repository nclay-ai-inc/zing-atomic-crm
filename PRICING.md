# ZingIQ Pricing Tiers

ZingIQ is a business intelligence platform for small businesses.

## Pricing Plans

### ZingIQ 100 - "Get Started"
- **Price:** $79/month
- **Target:** Solo entrepreneurs
- **Tagline:** Perfect for solo entrepreneurs who want powerful AI insights without the complexity

### ZingIQ 130 - "Scale Up" (MOST POPULAR)
- **Price:** $179/month
- **Target:** Growing teams
- **Tagline:** Built for growing teams ready to supercharge their customer relationships

### ZingIQ 160 - "Dominate"
- **Price:** $379/month
- **Target:** Established SMBs, agencies
- **Tagline:** For established SMBs and agencies who demand enterprise-grade intelligence

## Notes

- Pricing tier names follow the pattern: ZingIQ + number (100, 130, 160)
- Each tier includes progressively more features and higher usage limits
- All tiers include core CRM functionality plus business intelligence features
